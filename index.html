* How to Breathe
    * Box Breathing Technique
  1. Breathe in as the first layer of petals grows/fades
  2. Hold your breath as the second layer of petals grows/fades
  3. Slowly exhale through your mouth as the third layer of petals grows/fades
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 for future layers
  5. When the yellow pistil is complete, breathe in when the bowl rings and the outermost layer starts to fade
  6. Repeat steps 1-4 for future layers until you feel re-centered
