Breathing Exercise
    —Box Breathing Technique—
  1. Breathe in as the first layer of petals grows
  2. Hold your breath as the second layer of petals grows
  3. Slowly exhale through your mouth as the third layer of petals grows
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 for future layers until you feel re-centered
